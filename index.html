NEW THINGS TO COME

We are renovating! Previously known as Panorama Lounge, the 51st floor of the Manulife Centre will relaunch in March 2015 as The One Eighty. A palette of cool greys, warm woods and rich metals will complement the venue with a stylish yet modern elegance. A showpiece bar with built-in DJ station and chef showcase along with a glossy white barrel-vaulted ceiling will also highlight the space. A completely revamped food & cocktail menu will focus on comfort classics with a stylish twist.

We are excited to have you visit us, please return back to our website for updates on the date of our Grand Opening!
